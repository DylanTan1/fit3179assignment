Greenhouse Gas Emission Visualisations

The map below shows total green house gas emisions for each country in the year 2021. We can see that countries such as China, United States, India and Russia are the highest producers of greenhouse gases.

This chart shows the yearly global C02 generation and growth, in million tonnes. Aside from 2009 and 2020, every year, C02 emission is increasing at a concerning rate.

This bump chart shows the rank of the 10 biggest contributors of global warming. We can see that countries such as China, United States and Russia are still at the top of the rank.

Finally, this stacked bar chart breaks down the greenhouse gas emission into carbon dioxide, methane and nitrous oxide. China and United States have emitted much greater greenhouse gas through the 21 years, almost producing greater than the other countries in the chart combined.
